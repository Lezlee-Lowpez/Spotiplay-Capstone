How to Play

Welcome to SpotiPlay, where your Spotify song savvy is put to the test. Ready for a rapid-fire round of musical discovery? Here’s how to hit the high score in five tries:

  1. Start the Beat: Click 'Start' and brace yourself. You’ve got some clips and limited chances to shine.

  2. Tune In: Listen carefully as each song snippet plays. The clues are in the melody, waiting for you to catch them.

  3. Quick Guess: Identify the song? Type it in and submit your guess. Remember, you have limited shots, so make them count!

  4. Rack Up Points: Each correct guess boosts your score. Aim for accuracy and secure your spot on the leader board.

  5. Five and Fly: After all the clips, the game wraps. Check your score, see where you stand, and discover new tracks along the way.

SpotiPlay isn’t just a game; it's a sprint through the sound waves. Find your rhythm, enjoy the music, and maybe even discover your new favorite track. Ready, set, SpotiPlay!
